---
title: Ersta Sjukhus — New Hospital
description: A ~23,000 m² hospital on Södermalm, built on a steep hillside between Folkungagatan and Fjällgatan — Tengbom took over the design in 2017 from Nyréns Arkitektkontor and carried it through to completion in 2023.
publishDate: "2026-03-01"
lang: en
tags: [Revit, Facade, Healthcare]
coverImage: /images/projects/ersta/01-revit-model-front.jpg
featured: false
category: Practice
role: "Architect — Facade & Model Rebuild"
context: "Tengbom · Stockholm"
timelineLabel: "2017 — 2021"
awards: ["Best Healthcare — Monocle Design Awards 2024", "Finalist — Årets Stockholmsbyggnad 2024"]
problem: "Tengbom inherited the Ersta Sjukhus design in 2017 from Nyréns Arkitektkontor as an IFC export — reverse-engineering another firm's design intent into a production-ready Revit model, on a steep hillside site with 38,000 m³ of rock to blast and listed heritage structures to integrate around."
solution: "I rebuilt the entire Revit model from scratch based on the inherited IFC, then became primary modeler for the building's facade — the ocher brown anodized aluminum cladding system that defines the hospital. I coordinated weekly with electrical, HVAC, and structural teams, and joined fabrication meetings to validate the facade could actually be manufactured and installed as designed."
techStack: [Revit Architecture, IFC, Navisworks, Solibri]
impact: "The completed hospital won Best Healthcare at the Monocle Design Awards 2024 and was a finalist for Årets Stockholmsbyggnad 2024. The facade — refined across dozens of coordination and fabrication meetings — became one of the building's defining features, and the project stands as proof of understanding a BIM model's full lifecycle: from inherited data, through design development, to what actually gets built."
